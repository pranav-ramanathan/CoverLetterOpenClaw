- Quantinuum positions itself as a full-stack quantum company combining high-fidelity hardware with advanced software.
- Mission/positioning language emphasizes practical impact, scientific rigor, and accelerating real-world quantum computing outcomes.

How this was translated into the letter:

1. **Company-specific hook**
   - Referenced integrated full-stack model and practical quantum acceleration.
2. **Technical fit**
   - Highlighted optimisation, ML experimentation, reliability testing, and HPC use.
3. **Evidence of execution**
   - Included quantified outcomes (latency and cost improvements).
4. **Interest-area alignment**
   - Explicitly referenced Quantum Software Development, Quantum Algorithms, QML/QNLP.
5. **Tone**
   - Technical, concise, non-generic, and grounded in verifiable accomplishments.
