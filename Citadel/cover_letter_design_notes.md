# Citadel SWE Cover Letter Design (applied)

This draft is built around what Citadel Securities emphasizes in the internship description:

1. **High-performing, resilient production technology**
   - Included concrete outcomes on latency and reliability.
2. **Short development cycles / rapid production impact**
   - Highlighted CI/CD and shipping in production.
3. **Large-data platforms and analytical rigor**
   - Included benchmarking, optimization, and reliability framework work.
4. **Programming + design strength**
   - Focused on engineering execution, architecture, and measurable impact.
5. **Probability/statistics familiarity**
   - Explicitly tied mathematics background to applied engineering judgment.
6. **Small-team collaboration in complex technical environments**
   - Included cross-functional/collaborative communication signal.

## Structure used
- Paragraph 1: Role fit + motivation
- Paragraph 2: Technical impact with metrics
- Paragraph 3: Analytical depth + communication
- Paragraph 4: Company-specific motivation + close

## Tone choices
- Direct, technical, measured
- No generic hype language
- Quantified impact wherever possible
